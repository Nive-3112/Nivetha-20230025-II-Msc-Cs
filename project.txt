Project: IPL DASHBOARD
Abstract: 1.This project presents an IPL dashboard designed for streamlined access to crucial cricket data. 
          2.It includes three interactive buttons—"Team," "Points," and "Venues"—allowing users to view
detailed team statistics, current points standings, and comprehensive venue information. 
          3.The dashboard aims to offer an intuitive and efficient way for cricket enthusiasts to stay updated with 
the latest IPL insights and analyses.
